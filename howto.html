How it Works

The concept of the System is through an Application Programming Interface (API), collecting news related to climate change from different sources of reliable news data of each region of interest and store this information in a centralized database , important to emphasize that the proposal is the collection only of hyperlinks of news and title and through techniques of data analysis (Big Data, Business Analytics, Natural Language Processing (NLP)) .

Provide users with a user-friendly web access interface and Executive Summary Reports by geographical area of interest
