Source News

Configure in the news collection API the reliable sources of each region of observation interest, whether through government portals, news agencies, multilateral development banks (WB, IDB, AFDB, ADB, AFD).

The variety of diverse and reliable news sources can be the key to the observatory's relevance to be a useful data source for strategic decision-making support.
